// 01 — Our origin

A private clinic built around one question: how long can we live well?

Founded in Zurich in 2009 by a team of physicians, geneticists, and translational researchers, Quantum Longevity was created to bring the rigor of academic medicine to the intimate scale of a private practice.

We don't promise miracles. We measure carefully, intervene precisely, and follow our members for decades. Healthspan is the goal; lifespan is the side effect.
