// Recovery & Energy

Whole Body Cryotherapy

Three minutes in a chamber cooled to sub-zero temperatures triggers a powerful systemic response: blood rushes to your core, inflammatory signaling drops, and your body floods with endorphins.

Members use it to shorten recovery after training, calm chronic inflammation, and reset on high-stress days. The cold is dry and brief — far more tolerable than an ice bath, and over before discomfort sets in.

  • Reduced muscle soreness and faster recovery
  • A sharp, clean energy lift that lasts hours
  • Lower systemic inflammation over a course of sessions

  • 20+ formulated nutrient and mineral blends

  • Drip selection guided by your diagnostics

// Healing & Cognition

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

Inside a pressurized chamber, you breathe pure oxygen at greater-than-atmospheric pressure. Your blood plasma carries far more oxygen than usual, reaching tissues that ordinary breathing struggles to supply.

HBOT supports wound healing, post-surgical recovery, and cognitive clarity — and offers a meaningful counter to the oxidative load of dense urban air. Sessions are calm and quiet; many members read or simply rest.

  • Accelerated healing and post-surgical recovery
  • Support for focus, clarity, and mental stamina
  • A restorative counter to urban air pollution

// Skin & Pain

Red Light Therapy

Specific wavelengths of red and near-infrared light penetrate the skin and stimulate the mitochondria in your cells — a process called photobiomodulation that supports energy production and tissue repair.

The result is visibly healthier skin, reduced inflammation, and meaningful relief for stubborn joint and muscle pain — all with zero downtime. You simply relax under the panels.

  • Skin renewal, tone, and collagen support
  • Reduced inflammation and joint pain relief
  • Completely non-invasive, with no downtime

// Strength & Composition

EMS Training

Electro-muscular stimulation sends gentle electrical impulses to your muscles while you move through a guided workout, activating deep fibers that conventional training rarely reaches.

Twenty supervised minutes delivers a training stimulus comparable to a far longer gym session — ideal for time-poor professionals working toward strength and body-composition goals.

  • A full-body stimulus in just 20 minutes
  • Activation of deep, hard-to-reach muscle fibers
  • Efficient strength and body-composition gains

// Detox & Calm

Far Infrared Sauna Therapy

Unlike a traditional sauna that heats the air around you, far infrared warms your body directly with radiant light. The heat is gentler and more tolerable, while reaching deeper into tissue.

The effect is a deep, productive sweat that supports circulation and detoxification, eases muscle tension, and — for many members — becomes a reliable way to downshift after a demanding day.

  • Improved circulation and a deep, gentle sweat
  • Support for detoxification and recovery
  • A calming, stress-reducing ritual
