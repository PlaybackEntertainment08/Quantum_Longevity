// Assessment

Two hours, one physician, the whole picture.

Before we measure anything, we listen.

Your physician spends a full two hours with you, in person, before a single lab is drawn. Family history. Prior bloodwork. Current medications. Lifestyle. Sleep. Stress. Goals — and what is non-negotiable for you.

The output is not a checklist. It's a working hypothesis about what is likely to matter most for your healthspan over the next decade — and a short list of the diagnostics most worth running.

// Diagnostics

200+ biomarkers. Read by a physician, not an algorithm.

A snapshot is useful. A baseline is the foundation.

We sequence your whole genome — not just SNP arrays — and pair it with a physical exam, body composition, VO₂ max, functional cardiac imaging, and a 200-marker panel covering metabolic, inflammatory, hormonal, and aging indices.

Every result is reviewed and interpreted by a member of your clinical team. You receive a written report and a 60-minute walkthrough — not a PDF in your inbox.

// Personalized Plan

Composed by humans — physicians, geneticists, nutritionists.

No two protocols look alike. The work to design yours is bespoke.

Your physician convenes a 90-minute internal review with the relevant specialists: the geneticist for variant interpretation, the clinical-nutrition team for dietary architecture, the performance team for training prescriptions. Specialists from our referral network join when indicated.

The plan is documented, ranked by expected impact, and explicit about what we will not do — and why.

// Monitoring

Continuous data, not annual snapshots.

Once we know your biology, we watch it carefully.

Continuous glucose monitoring, sleep architecture, HRV, and continuous activity are streamed to your private dashboard. Quarterly bloodwork keeps the deeper markers — apoB, ApoE, hs-CRP, methylation — in view.

Your physician reviews trends weekly during onboarding, then at a cadence you set together. If something is moving in a direction we don't like, we don't wait for next year's appointment.

// Optimization

Refine every 90 days. Repeat for decades.

The protocol that works today is unlikely to be the right protocol in five years.

Every 90 days, your team revisits the data, the diary, and the evidence base. Things that aren't working come off the protocol. New interventions enter when the literature — or your own biomarkers — warrant it.

Our average member relationship is nine years and counting. That length of partnership is the point: aging compounds slowly, and so does its reversal.
